WE ARE HERE TO RENEW THE SACRED

Who We Are

Nameless Mountain is a spiritual center based in Boulder, Colorado. We are focused on solving the unique challenges posed to human development and spiritual maturity in the 21st century.

Our meta-rational approach to spirituality is based in Dharma, fluent in contemporary psychology, reverent toward tradition, but wholly unconstrained by the past.

Founded in 2025 by Gabe Broussard, also known as Vivid Void, we are here to renew the sacred, whatever form it may take, wherever it may appear.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help people build strong connections to themselves, their bodies, their communities, and to the Sacred.

We accomplish this with meditation, Dharma-based instruction, contemporary spiritual practice and psychological education.

There are many spiritual centers that want to help people wake up.

We are devoted to helping people grow up.

Our Vision

We believe that the world needs a new kind of spiritual center. One that doesn't trade depth for accessibility or rigor for openness.

Our vision is to build a center where ancient contemplative wisdom meets contemporary developmental science. Where meditation practice is paired with psychological education. Where community is built not around belief, but around a shared commitment to growth.

We envision a place where people of all backgrounds can explore the deepest questions of human existence, supported by teachers who have walked the path themselves and by a community of fellow seekers.

Nameless Mountain will be a home for those who are ready to take their inner work seriously, who want to grow not just spiritually but as whole human beings, and who believe that the sacred is not something to be found only in temples and texts, but in every moment of our lives.

We are building something that doesn't quite exist yet. And we need your help to do it.
